Some standard weapons can be built. In order to know how, just go to a Generals Store and buy a manual which is sold in Weapon Recipes Shop.

Ingredients can be found in Weapon Construction Shops or Weapon Components Shops. Now you just have to follow manual's instructions.

The process is long because you have to find all ingredients, of nearly QL.

Here is a #L "List of Manuals" "/tell <myname> recipe 69" for Standard Weapons.
